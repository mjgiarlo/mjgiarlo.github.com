Automatic Text Retrieval Systems Design Questions

  • Problems with complex text representations

    • Related terms and phrases: Studies have repeatedly indicated that related terms and term phrases are inconsistent in terms of their effectiveness.
    • Thesauruses and knowledge bases: According to Salton, “viable procedures for the construction of effective vocabulary tools covering subject areas of reasonable scope appear to be completely lacking” due to a lack of consensus on their form and content.
