Digitization concerns

  • To outsource or not to outsource – human resources, funding, solid quality standards, trust. Perhaps a combination?

  • Setting up a digital imaging lab

    • Environment – dedicated room, color of walls (15% neutral grey), power/wiring requirements, climate control (W camera lighting effect on calibration), room security
    • Hardware – imaging equipment (format size), ADF if mostly standard-sized print, high-end workstation, storage concerns (removable media?)
    • Software – scanning & imaging device control, image editing (brightness, balance, sharpening, cropping)
