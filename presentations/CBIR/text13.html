Results

  • Both the COB and the POB outperformed the MQS

  • The COB and POB scored comparably with one another, except for part 3 of the qualitative analysis which recorded values on the relevance, importance, usefulness, appropriateness, and completeness of the images retrieved, where the COB clearly took the edge
