Why a “digital highway?”

  • Why not a “digital turnpike?”

  • The current state of digital project development reveals many disparate projects, services, and collections, each of which is effectively an “information island” surrounded by barriers to user-friendliness, discoverability, interoperability, and sustainability.

  • A “digital highway” links together these information islands, providing a centralized access point, an object model, and standard metadata.

  • Most importantly, a “digital highway” connects users to information.
