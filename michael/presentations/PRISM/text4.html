Preservation and Metadata

  • FEDORA leverages a simple but elegant model to package together the following components of a digital object:

    • A unique, persistent identifier
    • Descriptive and administrative metadata of any schema, although METS and Dublin Core are native
    • Disseminators, to preserve the object's behavior
    • The object itself, called a “data-stream” in FEDORA-speak
