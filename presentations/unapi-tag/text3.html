A short genealogy of unAPI

  • unAPI has its roots in two well-known library specifications: OpenURL and OAI-PMH

  • Grandpappy: ContextObjects in Spans (COinS), developed in the first two quarters of 2005

  • Mama: COinS-PMH, developed in the last two quarters of 2005

  • The COinS-PMH effort led directly to unAPI, which was developed between late fall 2005 and finalized in June 2006, with interim versions released on a monthly basis (almost)
